Research Methods and Professional Practice

DEVELOPING RESEARCH SKILLS FOR CYBER SECURITY

Module Overview

This module is a practical guide to research methods, giving you the tools to conduct research in a professional setting. It covers various techniques and strategies for effective research, focusing on real-world applications. It also provides crucial insight into the legal, social, ethical, and professional issues that apply to computing projects, teaching you how to conduct research responsibly and in accordance with relevant codes of practice..

Key Learning Objectives

  • Acquire a foundational understanding of research principles and methods based on the scientific method. This will prepare you to apply these concepts to various fields.
  • Developing research questions and hypotheses
  • Explore different research strategies and designs, learning how to select the most appropriate approach for your specific projects.
  • Develop practical research competencies, particularly in data collection and analysis. You'll learn to critically design and evaluate your own independent research.
  • Reflect on your learning journey, taking an independent and thoughtful approach to the material.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Appraise the professional, legal, social, cultural and ethical issues that affect computing professionals.
  • Appraise the principles of academic investigation, applying them to a research topic in the applicable computing field.
  • Evaluate critically existing literature, research design and methodology for the chosen topic, including data analysis processes.
  • Produce and evaluate critically a research proposal for the chosen topic.
  • Applying research ethics in cyber security

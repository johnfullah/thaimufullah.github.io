About Me

A seasoned IT Security consultant with over a decade of dedicated experience, specialising in highly regulated industries. Certified in ISO 27001, CISA, CISM, and CRISC, demonstrating a robust foundation in information and cyber security risk management, technology resilience, and Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) across on-premises and cloud environments.

Proficient in conceptualising and implementing security regulatory and compliance frameworks, Cloud Controls Assurance (CCA), IAM, PAM, strategies, roadmaps, and industry best practices in both private and public sectors.

Recognised for adept leadership – both hands-on and managerial – I excel in guiding cross-functional teams through diverse workstreams. My strategic approach ensures the successful execution of change initiatives that directly impact business-focused outcomes.
